# Gather (gather.town)

**First seen:** 2026-06-26 (v2 onboarding scan)
**Tier:** 3 — Context only
**Evidence quality:** DIRECT_OBSERVATION (WebFetch gather.town/features, pricing, use-cases) + HELP_ARTICLE + MARKET_RESEARCH. No live screenshots.

**URL:** https://www.gather.town
**Category overlap:** Medium — captures the "virtual presence + spontaneous conversation" slice, but positioning has shifted to enterprise remote teams, leaving the student case largely unserved.
**Business model:** Per-seat SaaS, $12/user/mo annual ($15 monthly). 30-day trial (no permanent free tier). Education discount on request only. ~$10.8M ARR (2024, third-party estimate).

## Key UX patterns
1. **Proximity-based spatial audio/video** — avatars on a 2D map; conversation starts in radius (mimics in-person). Core differentiator. [HELP_ARTICLE]
2. Unlimited async chat + live spatial presence in one workspace (Gather 2.0). [DIRECT_OBSERVATION]
3. AI meeting notes/transcription bundled into Essential. [DIRECT_OBSERVATION]
4. **No offline functionality** documented; connectivity is a hard requirement. [HELP_ARTICLE]
5. **No education-specific tooling** — education page is generic virtual-workspace marketing. [DIRECT_OBSERVATION]

## Pricing
Free: 30-day trial only (≤50 members). Essential $12/user/mo annual. Custom enterprise. Education discount request-only, no public rate.

## Strengths
Genuinely novel "hallway serendipity" spatial AV (some institutional study-hall/office-hours use); persistent async chat reduces scheduling overhead; strong multi-sharer screen share; avatar/space customization.

## Weaknesses / where StudyHall wins
- **No permanent free tier** — $12/user/mo is prohibitive for student groups.
- Zero academic tooling; fully online-dependent (no graceful degradation); **actively repositioning to corporate** (education appears abandoned); student-data privacy (FERPA/minors) undocumented; 2D-map setup friction for ephemeral sessions.

## Voice/video
Full real-time proximity spatial audio/video as core mechanic; Spotlight broadcast; multi-share screen sharing; recording on paid plans.
