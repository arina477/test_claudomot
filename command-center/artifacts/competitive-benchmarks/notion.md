**First seen:** 2026-06-26 (v2 onboarding scan)
**Tier:** 2 — Secondary / informative
**Evidence quality:** DIRECT_OBSERVATION (WebFetch notion.com education/pricing/releases/help) + MARKET_RESEARCH. One COULD_NOT_VERIFY (FERPA). No live screenshots.

**URL:** https://www.notion.com
**Category overlap:** Medium-high for async academic organization (notes, assignment trackers, schedules, shared workspaces); near-zero for real-time communication. Students pair Notion (content) + Discord (comms) — StudyHall targets the intersection Notion can't occupy alone.
**Business model:** Freemium, viral bottoms-up. Free for individuals; **Plus free for verified students/educators** (school email); paid Plus $10/seat, Business $20, Enterprise custom. AI bundled into Business/Enterprise (2026). ~$600M ARR, $10B valuation, 2M+ student licenses.

## Key UX patterns
1. **Document-as-database + huge template ecosystem** — assignment trackers, class schedules, GPA calculators out of the box. Strongest student hook (zero-setup structure). [DIRECT_OBSERVATION]
2. **Real-time co-editing** (cursors, @mentions, comments) but **no chat/voice/video/notification layer** — communication is async via mentions/comments. [DIRECT_OBSERVATION]
3. **Offline mode shipped Aug 2025** (2.53): manual page pinning; Recents/Favorites auto-download on Plus+. Core blocks offline, coverage incomplete; real-time collab needs connectivity. [DIRECT_OBSERVATION]
4. Free student Plus: one workspace, all org members, ≤100 guests, 30-day history, unlimited uploads — materially useful cohort tier at $0. [DIRECT_OBSERVATION]
5. Flexibility → choice paralysis / setup time; students commonly run Notion + Discord together. [MARKET_RESEARCH]

## Pricing
Free: unlimited personal pages, 10 guests, 7-day history, 5MB cap. Plus free for verified students; $10 others. Business $20 (AI). Enterprise custom.

## Strengths
Dominant in student productivity (2M+ licenses, free Plus removes price barrier); best-in-class async academic organization (assignment tracking, semester planning, linked DBs) — functionality StudyHall hasn't built; offline mode exists (partial); massive template community; well-funded, AI-investing.

## Weaknesses / where StudyHall wins
- **No real-time communication** — no chat/voice/video; students must run Discord/Slack alongside. StudyHall owns the comms layer Notion deliberately omits.
- Offline mode partial/new (pinned pages, core blocks, auto-download paid-only); **no study-group presence/session tooling** (can't show who's studying now, no voice study rooms); AI locked off free student tier; student-specific privacy/FERPA unconfirmed [COULD_NOT_VERIFY]; high-investment template-first onboarding.
