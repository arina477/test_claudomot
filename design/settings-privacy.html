Your data

StudyHall stores your profile info, messages, and academic activity. Review it, or download a copy for yourself.

What data do we store about you?

We only store what is required to provide the StudyHall experience. We do not partner with data brokers or advertisers.

  • Profile Information Your username, display name, avatar, chosen accent color, encrypted password hash, and active email address.
  • Message History (Server-side Encrypted) Text messages and media attachments sent within study servers, retained until you or the server organizer deletes them.
  • Academic Interactions Assignments marked complete, voice room session metadata (join/leave times, but never recordings), and server membership lists.

Delete your account

Permanently delete your profile, sever all server associations, and wipe your data. This action requires email verification and initiates a 30-day grace period.

Review our full Privacy Policy or Terms of Service.

Request Data Export

We'll compile a highly structured .zip file containing your profile info, text messages, assignment states, and activity logs. This process runs in the background.

Delete Account

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Deleting your account will immediately remove you from all study servers. Your messages will remain but attribute to a "Deleted User" to preserve academic context for your peers. Your personal data forms will be wiped.
